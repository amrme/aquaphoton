AquaPhoton Academy

AquaPhoton Academy is an initiative started by AquaPhoton Co. with the goal of spreading the marine technology among aspiring college students in Egypt.

The academy consists of three sub-branches; A team that is run by students from Alexandria University, and two other academic sub-branches that help the community by offering courses, and organizing camps.
